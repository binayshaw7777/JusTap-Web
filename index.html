FAQs

Q: What is Justap?

Justap is a mobile app designed to simplify the process of exchanging contact information and social links. It replaces traditional business cards with QR codes, making it easier and more secure to connect with others.

Q: How does Justap work?

Using Justap is simple. When you meet someone and want to exchange contact information, simply open the app and tap on the "JusTap" button. This will generate a unique QR code containing all your important details. The other person can then scan the QR code using their Justap app, and your information will be instantly shared.

Q: Can I exchange multiple contacts and social links with Justap?

Absolutely! Justap allows you to exchange multiple contacts and social links effortlessly. You can include various details such as phone numbers, email addresses, social media profiles, and more. Simply add them to your profile, and they will be included in the QR code.

Q: Is my information secure with Justap?

Yes, Justap prioritizes the security of your information. All data exchanged through the app is encrypted to ensure your privacy. Additionally, Justap does not store any of your personal data on its servers, providing you with a secure and private experience.

Q: What platforms is Justap available on?

Justap is currently available for Android devices. You can download it from the Google Play Store and start using it right away. Click here to download it from Playstore

Q: Can I customize my Justap profile?

Absolutely! Justap allows you to customize your profile with a profile picture and banner picture. You can also add a personal bio or description to make your profile more unique and appealing.

Q: Can I use Justap at networking events?

Yes, Justap is ideal for networking events. Instead of carrying a stack of business cards, you can simply use Justap to exchange contact information with other attendees. It's a convenient and efficient way to connect with new people.

Q: What technologies are used to build Justap?

Justap is built using a combination of technologies. The app is developed using Kotlin, the official programming language for Android. It also utilizes Coroutines for asynchronous calls, Android Architecture Components for better app design, Firebase for backend services, ZXing for QR code generation, ML Kit for QR code scanning, and other libraries like Room, LiveData, Glide, and Material Components for Android.

Q: How do I download Justap?

Justap is currently available for download on the Google Play Store. Simply search for "Justap" in the Play Store, and you'll find the app. Tap on the "Install" button to download and install Justap on your Android device.
